Dr. Isabella Mendes is a highly qualified professional dedicated to excellence in dental care, specializing in veneers, oral rehabilitation, and implant-supported prostheses. With extensive experience and certifications in aesthetic and restorative dentistry, she combines technical expertise with a patient-centered approach to deliver personalized, high-quality treatments.

Her expertise in veneers allows patients to achieve a naturally beautiful smile through minimally invasive techniques, enhancing tooth shape, color, and harmony with precision. In oral rehabilitation, Dr. Mendes focuses on restoring function and aesthetics, ensuring long-term oral health and improved quality of life. For patients needing implant-supported prostheses, she utilizes advanced techniques to provide stable, durable solutions that restore comfort and confidence.

With a dedication to transforming smiles and enhancing well-being, Dr. Isabella Mendes offers a comprehensive, professional approach that blends expertise with compassionate care, delivering outstanding results in every aspect of dental treatment.

Prophylaxis and Prevention

Preventive care is the foundation of a healthy smile. Regular professional cleanings, fluoride treatments, and personalized hygiene guidance help prevent cavities, gum disease, and other oral conditions, ensuring long-term dental health and well-being.

Fixed Prostheses on Implants (Protocol Prostheses)

Implant-supported prostheses provide a permanent, functional, and aesthetic solution for tooth loss. Designed for durability, stability, and natural appearance, they restore the ability to chew, speak, and smile confidently.

Teeth Whitening: At-Home, In-Office, or Combined

Restore the beauty and functionality of your teeth with composite resin restorations. These tooth-colored fillings blend seamlessly with natural enamel, providing strength, aesthetics, and durability while preserving tooth structure.

Composite Resin Restorations

Restore the beauty and functionality of your teeth with composite resin restorations. These tooth-colored fillings blend seamlessly with natural enamel, providing strength, aesthetics, and durability while preserving tooth structure.

Ceramic Veneers or Contact Lenses

Transform your smile with ultra-thin ceramic veneers or contact lenses. Designed to enhance shape, color, and symmetry, these custom-made restorations offer long-lasting, natural-looking results with minimal tooth reduction.

Pure Ceramic Prostheses

Experience the highest level of aesthetics with pure ceramic prostheses. Free of metal components, these restorations provide exceptional translucency, mimicking the natural appearance of teeth while ensuring durability and biocompatibility.

Aesthetic Orthodontic Appliances

Straighten your teeth discreetly with aesthetic orthodontic solutions. Whether using clear aligners or ceramic braces, modern techniques ensure effective alignment while maintaining a natural and elegant look throughout treatment.

Specialized Pediatric Dentistry

Children's oral health requires special care. Our pediatric dentistry services focus on prevention, gentle treatments, and education, ensuring young patients develop strong, healthy smiles while feeling comfortable and confident.

Implant and Oral and Maxillofacial Surgery

Advanced surgical solutions for oral rehabilitation. From dental implant placement to complex maxillofacial procedures, our expertise ensures precise, safe, and effective treatments to restore function, aesthetics, and confidence.
